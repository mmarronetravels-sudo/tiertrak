# Annual MTSS Calendar

A working template for your building's **Tier 1 implementation calendar**. The calendar is the schedule your MTSS Design & Implementation Team commits to at the start of the year — screening windows, monthly team meetings, data reviews, and PD sessions. Having it on paper means conflicts get caught in August, not October.

Fill this out at the start of the year alongside the MTSS Handbook. Post the filled-in version where your team can reference it without digging. Revisit each section at the end of the year and carry the structure forward.

This template organizes events by **category** (how MTSS cadence is actually planned) rather than by month. A one-page summary table at the end pulls everything together for posting or printing.

## 3. Data review meetings

The team meetings immediately following each screening window where the team reviews screener results and decides on Tier 1 adjustments and Tier 2 referrals.

Often these are folded into the regular monthly team meeting; some schools schedule them as separate working sessions. Either way works — what matters is that the data gets looked at together, within two weeks of window close.

## Revisit cadence

- **Start of year:** fill out all sections; confirm dates with the full team before committing
- **End of year:** note what dates worked, what didn't (e.g., "winter data review was too close to holidays — push one week later next year")
- *(Optional) Mid-year check-in: confirm remaining dates still work; adjust for snow days or schedule changes*
